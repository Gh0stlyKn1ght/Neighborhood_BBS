### Community Contributors

Thank you to everyone who has contributed to Neighborhood BBS!

To add yourself to this list, please:

1. Fork the repository
2. Make your contribution
3. Submit a pull request
4. Your name will be added here!

### Ways to Contribute

- **Code:** Bug fixes, features, optimizations
- **Documentation:** Guides, tutorials, translations
- **Hardware:** Firmware for new platforms
- **Testing:** Bug reports, quality assurance
- **Design:** UI/UX improvements
- **DevOps:** Docker, deployment, infrastructure
